About Me



During my Master's degree studies in Computer Science at the University of Essex Online (UK), I had the opportunity to engage with cutting-edge coursework and learn from esteemed faculty members. I am grateful and glad to have studied at the University of Essex, and I believe that my time there has equipped me with a solid foundation in the field of computer science.

The University of Essex Online provided me with an exceptional learning environment, complete with state-of-the-art resources and a vibrant community of students from diverse backgrounds. I am appreciative of the experiences and knowledge I gained during my time there, and I continue to apply these skills and insights in my current role as a Technical Research Writer at HELPFUL Engineering.

I credit my success in large part to the rigorous academic curriculum and supportive learning environment that the University of Essex provided. I am confident that the skills and knowledge I gained there will serve me well throughout my professional career.
